REFUND POLICY - STRICT GUIDELINES

1. REFUND WINDOW
   - Defective/Wrong item: 30 days from purchase date
   - Change of mind: 7 days from purchase date
   - Wrong size: 15 days from purchase date

2. ELIGIBLE REASONS
   - Defective product
   - Wrong item sent
   - Wrong size delivered
   - Not as described

3. INELIGIBLE REASONS
   - Change of mind after 7 days
   - Used products (unless defective)
   - Missing original packaging

4. ORDER VALUE LIMITS
   - Refunds above Rs.10,000 require manager approval
   - Partial refunds allowed for orders above Rs.5,000

5. SPECIAL CASES
   - Allergic reactions: Eligible only with medical proof
   - Electronics: Must be unused and in original packaging

6. AUTO-DENY CONDITIONS
   - Purchase older than 30 days (any reason)
   - Customer has more than 2 previous refunds
   - Order currently in transit (not delivered)
